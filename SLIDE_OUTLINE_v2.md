## Slide 2: The Chain of Verifiable Cognition (Architecture)
**Visual:** A Flow/System Diagram (Left-to-Right).
*   **Step 1: The Stochastic Brain:** An LLM proposes a logical step ($S_t$).
*   **Step 2: The Deterministic Gate (RFL):** The MathLedger Engine checks $S_t$ against axioms.
    *   *If Invalid:* Feedback Loop (Red Arrow) sends error back to LLM.
    *   *If Valid:* Step is hashed and committed.
*   **Step 3: The Dual-Attestation Ledger:**
    *   **Proof 1:** Mathematical validity of the step.
    *   **Proof 2:** Cryptographic consensus of the sequence (Merkle Root).
*   **Outcome:** An immutable "Certificate of Reasoning" attached to the final output.
